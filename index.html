HOW IT WORKS

1 - SEARCH

Search for items that you need. Choose the store that offers the best price and is closest to you

2 - FILTER

Filter your search based on order type, item categories, pricing and distance

3 - CONTACT

Get in touch with the personnel and ask for delivery time. Give them your address and phone number. Order online or via phone, whichever is preferable

4 - RECEIVE

Receive your essentials at your doorstep. Make your payment if you chose cash on delivery
